About me

I am a usage-based linguist and my research spans Cognitive Linguistics, Sociolinguistics, and Historical Linguistics, with a focus on Romance languages. Central to my approach is the integration of speaker-focused perspectives and authentic data to bridge cognitive and sociolinguistic research. I am passionate about designing research-led curricula, and I am committed to supporting students’ well-being, diversity, and work-life balance.
